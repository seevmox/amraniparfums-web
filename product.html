Nuit d'Orient is a fragrance for those who are drawn to the shadows. It opens with a luminous burst of saffron and black pepper, before descending into a rich heart of aged Hindi oud and Bulgarian rose. The base is a dark meditation — sandalwood and black amber smoldering beneath a veil of musks. This is not a fragrance for the faint of heart. It is a declaration.
Concentration: Eau de Parfum (22%)

Volume: 50 ml / 1.7 fl oz

Origin: Created in Grasse, France with Moroccan oud

Bottle: Hand-blown artisan glass with 24k gold collar

Longevity: 12–16 hours on skin

Sillage: Intense
