Our Story

La Maison

A Vision Born
from Passion

AMRANI PARFUMS was founded on a singular belief: that fragrance is the most intimate form of luxury. Not worn on the surface, but carried invisibly — a second skin that speaks before words do.

Rooted in the rich perfumery traditions of Morocco and the Arab world, and elevated by a deep appreciation for French haute parfumerie, our house occupies a rare space between two worlds — where Eastern opulence meets Western precision.

Every fragrance we create begins with a story. A memory. A landscape. An emotion so powerful it demands to be bottled. We work with master noses across Grasse, Rabat, and Dubai to translate these intangible moments into olfactory experiences that endure.

Principles

What We Stand For

I

Rare Authenticity

We refuse to compromise. Every ingredient is sourced from its origin — Bulgarian rose from the Valley of Roses, oud from the forests of Hindi, vetiver from the soil of Haiti. No substitutes. No shortcuts.

II

Timeless Craft

In an era of mass production, we choose the slow path. Each fragrance undergoes years of refinement before it earns the right to wear our name. Patience is our most valuable ingredient.

III

Invisible Luxury

True luxury whispers. It does not announce itself — it is discovered. Our fragrances are designed for those who understand the difference between wearing a scent and living within one.

The Art of
Composition

Perfumery is not a science — it is a dialogue. Between the perfumer and the material, between the past and the present, between the creator and the wearer who will one day make the fragrance their own.

Our collections are built around contrast: the warm and the cool, the soft and the sharp, the ancient and the modern. It is in this tension that beauty is born.

Meet the Visionary

Mohamed El Amrani

Founder & Creative Director, AMRANI PARFUMS

Fès, Morocco · Open to Collaborations

Mohamed El Amrani is a cybersecurity student and self-taught entrepreneur who turned a lifelong obsession with scent into a luxury fragrance house. Growing up in Fès — one of the world's oldest perfume cities — he was surrounded by the alchemy of spice markets, artisan distilleries, and centuries of olfactory tradition.

At 18, Mohamed founded AMRANI PARFUMS with a simple mission: to create fragrances worthy of the world's most discerning noses, while staying true to the soul of his heritage. He brings to the brand an obsessive attention to detail, a designer's eye for aesthetics, and a hacker's instinct for seeing what others miss.
